## The Death (One Sentence)
A trusting gap year traveler is murdered by her travel companion of three months who has been planning to steal her inheritance money and disappear.

## Tagline
"Some friendships are made to last. Others are made to kill."

## Themes
1. The dangerous intimacy of travel friendships - satirizes how quickly strangers become "best friends" on the road
2. The vulnerability of sharing everything with someone you barely know
3. Class differences and financial resentment among travelers
